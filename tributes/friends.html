TRIBUTE TO A DEPARTED FRIEND AND COLLEAGUE.

'Oti gwom gwom'!
This was how we, your friends and colleagues, used to call you because you were always full of life.
I still remember vividly the beautiful times we had while working in Federal Ministry of Works, Headquarters, Road Construction and Rehabilitation Division ( south- west), Abuja.
This was prior to our meritorious retirement from service. 'Oti gwom gwom', my heart bleeds as I drop this short Tribute.
It is, indeed, very hard to believe that you are no more: a man full of life and kindness, an accomplished Civil Engineer, an excellent colleague and friend, a respected and responsible gentleman, a loving and doting husband to his wife, children and all.
You have fulfilled your purpose in life and on earh and we will always share your stories and cherish them.
We miss you so much but God loves you more. May your beautiful soul rest with the Lord in perfect peace until we meet again to part no more.
ADIEU!!

Sir ( Engr.) Felix Nnamdi Nwokedi (KSJI).


We received with great shock the death of your beloved husband, father, grandfather, and our friend Engr.
James A. Otiji. During our interactions with him, we were graced with his warmth and sense of humour. He radiated love, peace, and positively affected the lives of those who encountered him.
Oby, I feel very much for you, cognizant of the wonderful partnership you both had as husband and wife.
We will miss his jokes and infectious smile, but we will all be consoled because he lived a fulfilled life and did not go through the pains of a protracted illness which was always his prayer.
During this trying moment, we pray for God's love, strength and protection. We also pray to God to give the entire Otiji family the fortitude to bear this irreparable loss.
We join you in prayer that God will grant his kind and peaceful soul eternal rest. Amen.
Accept our Condolences.

Dr. (Mrs) Nkiru Okpalanze ( on behalf of Engr. Nwabu. Okpalanze family)


TRIBUTE TO A GREAT MAN

Indeed a great iroko tree has fallen !
A great lion has been trapped!!
A rare gem and a hero is gone !!!
A man of the people an active and peaceful man, full of wisdom , nobility, humility, gentility , simplicity and a philanthropist full of philosophical saying , all worthy of emulation, would be perceived no more. Death thou art inevitable indeed .
You sneaked in and snatched away my great uncle Eng JAMSE OTIJI EZE AMANA OGAECHI the iroko tree that stood unique amidst the forest. the hand that fed thousand of mouths .
A self made man who grew to care more for other people's problems than his private needs Death what gave thee the brevity to intercept to the joyous mood of our kit and kin and substitute it with mood emotions? Men are lamenting, women are wailing, and children, are weeping .
Our family has lost a rare gem , for the legacy bequeathed by eze Amana OGAECHI may be extra difficult to acquire . Great uncle remain in GODs own bosom.
Adieu pa James ! Adieu ikenga!! Adieu eze Amana
OGAECHI Eng CHIDERA .FT NNABUIFE
