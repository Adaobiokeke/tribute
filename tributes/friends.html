TRIBUTE TO A DEPARTED FRIEND AND COLLEAGUE.

'Oti gwom gwom'!
This was how we, your friends and colleagues, used to call you because you were always full of life.
I still remember vividly the beautiful times we had while working in Federal Ministry of Works, Headquarters, Road Construction and Rehabilitation Division ( south- west), Abuja.
This was prior to our meritorious retirement from service. 'Oti gwom gwom', my heart bleeds as I drop this short Tribute.
It is, indeed, very hard to believe that you are no more: a man full of life and kindness, an accomplished Civil Engineer, an excellent colleague and friend, a respected and responsible gentleman, a loving and doting husband to his wife, children and all.
You have fulfilled your purpose in life and on earh and we will always share your stories and cherish them.
We miss you so much but God loves you more. May your beautiful soul rest with the Lord in perfect peace until we meet again to part no more.
ADIEU!!

Sir ( Engr.) Felix Nnamdi Nwokedi (KSJI).
