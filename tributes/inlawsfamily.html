TRIBUTE TO MY IN-LAW ENGR.JAMES ANIESODO OTIJI

The news of your transition, came to me and the entire family leaving me completely devastated especially when it occurred to me that I would no longer see or hear from you on the phone as usual.
It however made me reflect that life is a transient gift from God and before you know it, it is all over.
Engr Aniesodo, you lived a fulfilled life worthy of emulation for us the living because you touched many lives both in your village and Umuabi-udi and the Country as a whole even after you left public Service as a Director.
Engr. we wish you well as you embark on this Journey of meeting your creator.
We pray he will have mercy on you and we Umuabi Indigines wish you a safe journey your Community will Miss you.

FROM COMRADE C Ezenwaoriaku (OCHO -UDO) UMUABI
