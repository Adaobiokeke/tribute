A TRIBUTE TO MY IN-LAW, CHIEF, ENGR. JAMES ANIESODO OTIJI

It's said that any good and responsible man can be a father even if you are not related to that person by blood and I totally agree with that, as my in-law lived in attestation to that.
The news of your demise came so shocking to us. Just barely three months ago I sat in discussion with you as one family, little did I know what fate has in store for us.
Yet, God is all knowing and we give him thanks in all situation.
Nna anyi, you were one in a million, with a heart of gold. your cheerful, caring, doting & benevolent nature is second to none.
Right from the time we came asking for your daughter's hand in marriage, you made everything easy for us. You never allowed my son to be troubled with anything.
The good legacies & positive values you inculcated into your family is worthy of celebration. A positive which remains a bane of our long and strong in-law relationship.
You were a devout christian and we are comforted knowing that your place is already assured besides our lord Jesus Christ in Heaven...
Until we meet to part no more, Continue to rest in the Bossom of the lord.
We miss you so much!!
Your's in-law
Ezinne Florence Okeke
On behalf of Okeke's family

TRIBUTE TO MY IN-LAW ENGR.JAMES ANIESODO OTIJI

The news of your transition, came to me and the entire family leaving me completely devastated especially when it occurred to me that I would no longer see or hear from you on the phone as usual.
It however made me reflect that life is a transient gift from God and before you know it, it is all over.
Engr Aniesodo, you lived a fulfilled life worthy of emulation for us the living because you touched many lives both in your village and Umuabi-udi and the Country as a whole even after you left public Service as a Director.
Engr. we wish you well as you embark on this Journey of meeting your creator.
We pray he will have mercy on you and we Umuabi Indigines wish you a safe journey your Community will Miss you.

FROM COMRADE C Ezenwaoriaku (OCHO -UDO) UMUABI
