A TRIBUTE TO AN ICON

GOOD NIGHT TO MY HUSBAND

My Jim, so sad to come to terms with the fact that you left your beloved children and I without a father and a husband.
The entire episode at the hospital seemed so unreal. I had never imagined seeing you go, the recurring decimal remain "could this be real? Does it mean the roaring of the Lion will henceforth seize?
Jim could you have gone with all those endowment from above just like that? What a puzzle. Your friend Engr. Akinbode Akinluyi often called you The Ahitophel of our time because your wisdom, knowledge and experiences Were unmatchable, you had answers to all questions but death.
The vacuum is already so glaring, we can only find solace in the noble and enduring legacies you left on the sands of time in many fields-- Your career, a responsible family man and by your Great impact of your life in your environment.
Lastly, though we can no longer enjoy your physical presence in our midst but the ideals for which you lived like a diamond will remain forever.
Remarkably, I personally take solace in Knowing that you gave your life to Jesus Christ before your demise.
Fare well My hearthrob

Your Wife
Grace Oby Otiji, PhD, NAEP, FICA.
