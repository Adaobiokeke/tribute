OPENING PRAYER

O God, to whom it belongs to have mercy and to spare, we humbly entreat you for the soul of your son LATE ANIESODO JAMES OTIJI, whom you have summoned from this world, that you would deliver him from the hands of the enemy and not forget him forever, but bid your Holy Angels to receive him and lead him to your home in paradise.
Since he has placed all his hope in you, may he not know the pains of hell but bring him eternal happiness in heaven, through Christ our Lord Jesus your son who lives and reigns with you and the unity of the Holy Spirit world without end, Amen.


FIRST READING

A Reading from the Book of Wisdom(3:1-9)

Brethren, the souls of the virtuous are in the hands of God, no torment shall ever touch them. In the eyes of the unwise, they did appear die, their going looked like a disaster, their leaving us, like annihilation; but they are in peace.
If they experience punishment as men see it, their hope was rich with immortality; light was their affliction, great will blessing be. God has put them to test and proved them worthy to be with Him. He has tested them like gold in a furnace, and accepted them as holocaust. When the time comes for His visitation, they will shine out: as sparks run through the stubble, so will they.
They shall judge nations, rule over peoples, and the Lord will be their King forever. They who trust in Him will understand the truth, those who are faithful will live with Him in love, for grace and mercy wait those He has chosen.


This is the word of the Lord.

Thanks be to God.

Gospel

A reading from the Holy Gospel according to Matthew(5:1-12)

When Jesus saw the crowds, he went up the mountain; and after he sat down. His disciples came to him. Then he began to speak, and taught them, saying: Blessed are the poor in spirit, for there is the kingdom of heaven.
Blessed are those who mourn, for they will be comforted. Blessed are the meek, for they will inherit the earth. Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for righteousness, for they will be filled.
Blessed are the merciful, for they will receive mercy. Blessed are the pure in heart, for they will see God. Blessed are the peace-makers, for they will be called children of God.
Blessed are those who are persecuted for righteousness sake, for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.
Blessed are you when people revile you and persecute you and utter all kinds of evil against you falsely on my account, Rejoice and be glad, for your reward is great in heaven, for in the same way they persecuted the prophets who were before you.

HOMILY

PRAYER OF THE FAITHFUL

Let us trustingly call upon God the Almighty Father who raised Christ, His Son, from the dead, for the salvation of the living and the dead.
1. For our departed brother, Late Aniesodo James Otiji, may he who receives the grace of Baptism, reap its fruits as he enjoys the happiness of eternal life. We pray O Lord.
2. For all who are gathered here to pray for our father Late Aniesodo James Otiji: That we may always live, act and speak in full realization that one day we shall be called upon to answer for our thoughts, words and actions. We pray O Lord.

V. Our brother Late aniesodo James Otiji, was washed clean in baptism and anointed with oil of salvation: give him fellowship with your saints. We ask this in faith.
R. Lord hear our prayer
V. He was nourished with your Body and Blood, grant him a place at the table in your Heavenly kingdom. We ask this in faith
R. Lord hear our prayer
V. Comfort us in sorrow at the death of our brother Aniesodo James Otiji, let our faith be our consolation and eternal life our hope. We ask in faith.
R Lord hear our prayer.


LET US PRAY

Show Your mercy Lord to your departed servant LATE Aniesodo James Otiji, since he strove to do Your will, let him not be punished for his wrong doing.
And as he was united I through faith with all Your faithful people, let him now, by Your loving goodness, be united with the angelic throne.
Through Christ our Lord.


V. Eternal rest grant him O Lord
R. And let perpetual light shine on him.
V. May he rest in peace
R. Amen.


PRAYER FOR THE MOURNERS

Grant, O God, that while we mourn the departure of this your servant Late Aniesodo James Otiji, we may always remember that we most certain to follow him. Give us grace to prepare for the last hour by a good life, that we may not be surprised by a sudden and unprovided death but be ever watchful that when you call we may enter into eternal glory. Through Christ our Lord.

FOR THE DECEASED

May Mary, the most Virgin Mother of God, Kindest comforter to those who mourn, commend to her Son, the soul of her servant Late Aniesodo James Otiji, who has departed this life, that through her maternal intercession, he may quickly reach his longed for home in the heavenly fatherland, and live forever and ever. Amen.
V. May the soul of brother Late Aniesodo James Otiji and the faithful departed.
R. Through the mercy of God rest in peace.
Amen.
